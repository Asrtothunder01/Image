How to Use TraceWorks:

  • Select a tool from the toolbar: Draw, Erase, or Rectangle.
  • Use the "Choose Color" tool to select your preferred drawing color.
  • Upload an image using the "Upload Image" button.
  • Make annotations on the canvas with your chosen tool.
  • Click "Save" to download your work or "Clear" to reset the canvas.
